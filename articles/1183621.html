Russian rouble edges up supported by expected forex sales

brecorder 2023-09-08 08:47:53
Source page
The Russian rouble rose on Friday helped by expectations of sales of foreign currency by exportoriented companies and the central bank At 0810 GMT the rouble was 033 stronger against the dollar at 9794 and had gained 045 to trade at 10494 to the euro It had firmed 08 against the yuan to 1331 The central bank has said it will sell 150 billion roubles worth of forex in total between Sept 14 and Sept 22 equating to 214 billion roubles a day up from 23 billion roubles previously It linked the move to the planned redemption of 3 billion worth of Russian eurobonds on Sept 16 saying holders would be paid in roubles that some of them were expected to seek to convert into foreign currency Brent crude oil a global benchmark for Russias main export was down 01 at 8982 a barrel Russian stock indexes declined in early trade The dollardenominated RTS index was down 07 to 1007 points The roublebased MOEX Russian index was 11 lower at 3134 points
