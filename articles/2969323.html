Limerick's Sean Finn steps out with girlfriend Karen Kinnerk at teammates star studded wedding

rsvplive 2023-09-11 12:34:39
Source page
Injured Limerick hurler Sen Finn stepped out with his long time girlfriend Karen Kinnerk over the weekend. The couple were in attendance as GAA hero Declan Hannon married Louise Cantillon in a sun filled wedding in Adare Manor on Friday afternoon. Sen missed out on Limerick's four in a row All-Ireland Senior Hurling Championship win after he suffered a cruciate ligament injury during their defeat to Clare in the Munster round robin series in the Gaelic Grounds back in May. Read more: Limerick hero Cian Lynch poses with girlfriend Dayna at Declan Hannon's lavish wedding Sen and Karen turned up the style stakes as the posed outside the famous 5* resort. He looked handsome in a black tuxedo and black sunglasses while she wowed in figure hugging royal blue dress. She paired the look with matching opened toes heels and a classic black handbag. The Sunday Game pundit Shane Dowling was also on the guest list and he stepped out with his fianc Paula Donnelly. The RT star stuck to the black tie dress code while his belle wore green number complete with a statement gold head piece. The former Limerick hurler got engaged while enjoying a break in the luxurious Adare Manor back in December. Paula's gorgeous engagement ring could be seen as the husband and wife-to-be looked lovingly at each other in their announcement post on Instagram at the time. "A Christmas like no other to be had," he wrote at the time. Also at GAA wedding of the year were Limerick favourites Kyle Hayes, Barry Nash, Mike Casey, Aaron Gillane, Gearoid Hegarty and Dan Morrissey.
