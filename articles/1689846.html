Man Utd boss jumps to defence of Harry Maguire amid disrespectful criticism

manchesterworld 2023-09-15 14:30:06
Source page
Manchester United are back in Premier League action this weekend as they target three points boss Erik ten Hag has said under-fire defender Harry Maguire doesnt deserve the criticism from the media and fans. The centre-back has had a tough time of it of late and scored an own goal for England in their most recent game against Scotland. The former Leicester City man was linked with a move away from Old Trafford in the last transfer window with West Ham among the club mentioned as potential suitors. However, he ended up staying put beyond the deadline earlier this month.
