Virat Kohli's Playful Stint As Water Boy Amuses Spectators During India Vs Bangladesh Match In Asia Cup 2023

in_mashable 2023-09-15 13:20:15
Source page
Virat Kohli, the charismatic leader of the Indian cricket team, is not just a force on the field but also a source of joy and laughter, even when he's not actively playing. The recent Asia Cup 2023 Super Four match against Bangladesh showcased his knack for injecting humor into the game. In this particular match, where Kohli was given a well-deserved break, he didn't sit idly on the sidelines. Instead, he assumed the role of a water boy for his team, and it was anything but ordinary. After the dismissal of Anamul Haque, Kohli burst onto the field, racing with bottles of water for his teammates. His distinctive and energetic running style left fans in stitches and added a delightful twist to the proceedings. Although Kohli wasn't on the field, India made a sensational start to the game, thanks to the early breakthroughs provided by Mohammed Shami and Shardul Thakur. Rohit Sharma, leading the team, won the toss and opted to bowl against Bangladesh.
