Nicolas Cage, 59, is effortlessly cool in a black zebra-print suit for his film premiere at TIFF... after revealing details about baby daughter's first birthday By Kevin Kayhart For Dailymail.com Published: 05:47 BST, 10 September 2023 | Updated: 08:56 BST, 10 September 2023 e-mail 95 shares 22 View comments Nicolas Cage had all eyes on him as he arrived at the premiere for his new comedy Dream Scenario during the 2023 Toronto International Film Festival on Saturday. Looking cool in an all-black animal print suit with a matching dress shirt, tie and shoes, the Hollywood A-lister, 59, exuded confidence as he made his way from his car to the venue. Cage who was one of just a few actors to land an interim agreement from SAG-AFTRA to attend the festival amid the actors' and writers' strikes offered friendly waves at fans and photographers before stepping onto the red carpet. Though he got in a slew of solo snaps, Cage made it a point to pose for photos with Dream Scenario's talented cast and crew ahead of the screening. For his latest role, Cage plays Paul Matthews, a hapless professor who finds his life turned upside down when millions of strangers suddenly start seeing him in their dreams. But ultimately his nighttime appearances take a nightmarish turn, which then forces Paul to navigate through his newfound stardom, according to IMDb. Along with Cage, the comedy film also stars Julianne Nicholson, Michael Cera, Tim Meadows, Dylan Gelula, Lily Bird, Dylan Baker and Jessica Clement. Dream Scenario is scheduled to have its wide released by A24 on November 10. While Cage is now focused on the promotional push for Dream Scenario, he's also directing as much love and attention as he can to his daughter August, who just celebrated her milestone first birthday. Just two days before blowing out her candles, the proud dad revealed that August had picked out a special outfit for her birthday. 'She's going to wear a Supergirl cape,' Cage told People at the opening of the store Philipp Plein. Cage shares his daughter with fifth wife Riko Shibata, 28, whom he married in February 2021. The Moonstruck star shared the exciting news that he was having a baby girl while promoting The Unbearable Weight Of Massive Talent on The Kelly Clarkson Show in April 2022. The actor, who is the nephew of famed director Francis Ford Coppola, also has two sons Weston, 32, and Kal-El, 17, from previous relationships. Cage has appeared in over 110 movies, dating back to the early 1980s when he was a young, eager artist trying to get a break. He would make made his acting debut in the television pilot he Best of Times (1981), but it was never picked up by ABC. After learning of his tax problems in 2009, Cage admitted he began to take just about any role he could get for the paycheck. By January 2010, the actor announced he would pay the IRS a large sum to settle all of his debts. 'Over the course of my career I have paid at least $70 million in taxes, unfortunately, due to a recent legal situation, another approximate $14 million is owed to the IRS,' Cage told People magazine at the time, as reported by NBC News . He continued, 'However, I am under new business management and am happy to say that I am current for 2009, all taxes will be paid including any to be determined state taxes.' He was able to tackle his IRS issues, mainly by starring in as many films as he could land since around 2009. Cage's Hollywood resume also includes Peggy Sue Got Married (1986), Wild At Heart (1990), Honeymoon In Vegas (1992), City Of Angels (1998), 8mm (1999), The Family Man (2000), Lord Of War (2004), World Trade Center (2006), Ghost Rider (2007), National Treasure: Book of Secrets (2007), Ghost Rider: Spirit Of Vengeance (2011), and The Croods (2013).
