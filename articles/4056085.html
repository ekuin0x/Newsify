Andy Farrells men launched their campaign by running in 12 tries during a thumping 82-8 demolition of Pool B minnows Romania. Ireland have pivotal Paris appointments against South Africa and Scotland on the horizon but feel they cannot afford to underestimate the nation sitting 15th in the Test rankings. Theyve got some of the best open field runners in world rugby, defence coach Simon Easterby said of Tonga. Theyve got guys in the forward pack that can mix it, they can offload. And theyve got attacking threats throughout their back line. Its a real challenge for us defensively, its one that were probably ready for after the Romania game. We didnt have to get through that much work without the ball. Its going to be a big step up from the challenge Romania threw at us.
