PepsiCo to Invest Rs 778 Crore in New Assam Plant Aims to Boost Local Economy

sentinel 2023-09-08 13:43:31
Source page
GUWAHATI PepsiCo India a leading name in the global food and beverage industry is embarking on a significant venture in the northeastern state of Assam India The company has unveiled plans to invest a substantial sum of Rs 778 crore in the establishment of a stateoftheart manufacturing plant in the Nalbari district of Assam This strategic move is not only a testament to PepsiCo s commitment to the Indian market but also a proactive step towards fostering economic development and sustainability in the region Spread across a sprawling 442acre expanse the new PepsiCo India manufacturing plant is poised to become a pivotal economic contributor to the local landscape Anticipated to be fully operational by 2025 this facility is expected to generate gainful employment opportunities for at least 500 individuals from the region This employment drive aligns with PepsiCos broader strategy to empower local communities and enhance employability in Assam One of the remarkable aspects of this venture is PepsiCo Indias pledge to procure a substantial quantity of 50000 tonnes of potatoes from Assambased sources These locally sourced potatoes will play a crucial role in the production of the companys beloved Lays chips further strengthening the bond between PepsiCo and Assams agricultural sector Eugene Willemsen the CEO of PepsiCos Africa Middle East and South Asia AMESA region expressed the significance of India as one of the fastestgrowing markets for the company in this expansive region He emphasized that the new manufacturing facility in Assam underscores PepsiCos enduring commitment to Indias economic development by nurturing an equitable and sustainable ecosystem Additionally PepsiCo India has entered into a tripartite Memorandum of Understanding MoU with the Assam Skill Development Mission and the Directorate of Employment and Craftsman Training This collaborative effort aims to bolster employability particularly among women in the region By creating diverse employment opportunities and empowering the local farming community PepsiCo India is aligning itself with the governments vision of achieving selfsufficiency in potato production The companys skilling initiatives as part of this partnership will focus on fostering economic empowerment among the women of Assam providing them with essential training and equal opportunities PepsiCos substantial investment in Assam reflects not only its corporate ambitions but also its dedication to supporting local economies sustainable agriculture and female empowerment in the region This venture is poised
