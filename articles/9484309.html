This is not a fringe movement: Tens of thousands turn out for Khalistan vote held at Surrey gurdwara

globalnews 2023-09-10 23:26:26
Source page
Tens of thousands of community members attended a vote that was held at a Surrey gurdwara on Sunday. The unofficial vote was to weigh support for an independent Sikh nation in northern India Khalistan. The vote is not officially binding in any way. The vote was held at the Guru Nanak Sikh Gurdwara the gurdwara where its former president Hardeep Singh Nijjar was shot and killed in mid-June. Nijjar was a staunch supporter of the independence movement. The vote is organized by Sikhs for Justice a group that is in full support of a Khalistan nation. There is no opposing voice or opposing group campaign. The group estimated more than 100,000 people attended the vote in Surrey.
