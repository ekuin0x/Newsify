Updates: Tributes to Nellie Boswell Bread actress Jean Boht as she dies aged 91

liverpoolecho 2023-09-13 10:32:57
Source page
Tributes are being paid to Wirral actress Jean Boht after her family confirmed she has died aged 91. , said: It is with overwhelming sadness that we must announce that Jean Boht passed away yesterday, Tuesday September 12. Jean had been battling vascular dementia and with the indefatigable spirit for which she was both beloved and renowned. She was a resident at Denville Hall, the home for members of the theatrical profession. Jean was born in Bebington, Wirral, and was most well known for playing Nellie Boswell in sitcom Bread. Today, after her family confirmed the sad news, people from the acting world, friends and family, have started to pay tribute to the popular actress.
