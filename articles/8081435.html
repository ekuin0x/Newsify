Novak Djokovic Wins Record 24th Grand Slam Title At US Open, Pays Tribute To Kobe Bryant

deadline 2023-09-11 00:11:41
Source page
Novak Djokovic defeated Daniil Medvedev to win the US Open and his 24th grand slam title. In the match against Medvedev, it took Djokovic three hours and 17 minutes to defeat his opponent 6-3, 7-6, 6-3. The Serbian tennis star is now tied with Margaret Court which they have held since the 1970s. As the tennis star made his way to the player box to celebrate, Djokovic pulled out a t-shirt to honor his friend Kobe Bryant . The graphic tee that said Mamba Forever had a photo of himself with the late basketball star.
