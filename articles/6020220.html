Oldpark Road accident: Car flipped on to its roof after North Belfast traffic incident

belfastlive 2023-09-10 20:13:54
Source page
Top of Alliance Avenue in North Belfast Police are currently in attendance after a car crash in North Belfast on Sunday evening. The accident happened at the Oldpark Road and Cliftonville Road roundabout at around 8.30pm. Images from the scene at the top of Alliance Avenue showed one car flipped on to its roof. It's believed one elderly man was taken to hospital and another driver is helping police with their enquiries. The roundabout was partially closed while recovery vehicles removed the overturned vehicle and drivers were urged to exercise caution when approaching the busy junction.
