Why should anyone care about condensed matter physics?

physicsworld 2023-09-10 09:45:47
Source page
Most people outside physics probably have no idea what condensed-matter science even means
Physicist and popular-science author James Kakalios is on a mission to show the public just how exciting and useful this field of physics can be
One of his key pieces of advice for would-be science communicators is to connect the outcomes of research with the everyday world
After all, without the humble semiconductor transistor, there would be no smartphones or personal computers
Healthcare technologies would be far less advanced, and you wouldnt even be able to start most cars on the road today
