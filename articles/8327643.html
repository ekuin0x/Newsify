The major factors driving the sand casting market are advancement in technology and cost-effectiveness. Tooling and Equipment International (TEI) has witnessed considerable changes in casting techniques. Sand casting has a long history and it has remained unchanged for years. However, things are changing now due to 3D printing. 3D printing for sand casting methods gives prominent results. For instance, in July 2020, ExOne launched a Sand 3D Printing Network which is prepared by 40 binder jet printing platforms in North America. Through this technology, ExOne S-Max and S-Print platforms provide high-quality 3D printed sand molds and cores to manufacturing industries in a short period. This technology is beneficial in removing human error which leads to advancement in the shapes of final products.
