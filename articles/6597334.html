Two men stabbed in front of families at Westfield Doncaster in Melbournes northeast

7news 2023-09-10 23:01:34
Source page
Two young men have been stabbed in front of shocked families at a major shopping centre in northeast. Emergency services were called after an altercation broke out between two groups of males at Westfield Doncaster at about 6.45pm on Saturday. CCTV obtained by 7NEWS shows one of the victims falling to the ground, then begin kicking towards his alleged attackers in defence. Watch the latest news and stream for free on A knife is then brandished at the 21-year-old victim. A second 21-year-old man was also stabbed and both were taken to hospital. One remains in a serious condition and the other has non-life-threatening injuries. The violent incident happened just metres from a mother and her young children sitting in the food court. Police have said the two groups of men were not believed to be known to each other. After the alleged stabbings, a 19-year-old Springvale man, a 17-year-old Mill Park teen and an 18-year-old Narre Warren man were arrested two streets away. They are assisting police with their inquiries and anyone with information is urged to contact police.
