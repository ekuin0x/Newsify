The mother-of-three, 27, welcomed twins, Summer and Star , with her partner Jared Bowen almost four months ago and is already back hitting the gym as she completed a personal training session. The former Love Islander cut a sport figure as she donned a pair of teal leggings and a multi-coloured patterned gym top. Leaving the gym, she threw on an oversized denim jacket as she made her way back to her car. The star carried her gym attired in a Nike drawstring bag thrown over her shoulder while her other essentials were in a Louis Vuitton 2,500 bum bag.
