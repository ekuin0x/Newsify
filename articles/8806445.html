Australia's Madison de Rozario wins Sydney Marathon women's wheelchair title at first attempt

Wheelchair racer Madison de Rozario has won the Sydney Marathon, with Sinead Diver and Brett Robinson claiming Australian titles. Australian wheelchair racing superstar Madison de Rozario has powered to victory in her debut outing at the Sydney Marathon.
