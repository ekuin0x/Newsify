Best Friend singer wears dress adorned with BONES at MTV Video Music Awards By Jacqueline Lindenberg For Dailymail.Com Published: 21:04 EDT, 12 September 2023 | Updated: 06:49 EDT, 13 September 2023 e-mail 20 shares 13 View comments Saweetie channeled the classic cartoon, The Flintstones, in a one-of-a-kind ensemble as she attended the 40th MTV Video Music Awards in Newark, New Jersey on Tuesday. The Best Friend hitmaker, 30, who recently made jaws drop at the Laquan Smith NYFW show earlier this week, joined other A-list celebrities at the ceremony - such as Demi Lovato and Doja Cat . The rapper donned a glittering, pastel pink sequin gown from Area's Fall/Winter 2023 couture runway. Bone embellishments were placed around the neckline as well as the waist for a unique flare to the look. Small cutouts could be seen on each side of the sleeveless gown that contained a hem that fell down to brush the ground. She slipped into a pair of open-toed, strappy heels and opted to slick back her dark locks into a ponytail, while loose strands were styled into waves that gently fell onto her forehead. Wow! Saweetie, 30, channeled the classic cartoon, The Flintstones, in a one-of-a-kind ensemble as she attended the 40th MTV Video Music Awards in Newark, New Jersey on Tuesday Unique ensemble: Bone embellishments were placed around the neckline as well as the waist of the dazzling dress for a unique flare to the look She accessorized her look for the special evening by wearing a pair of flashy, silver-hooped earrings as well as a chunky ring on her left hand. The performer - whose real name is Diamont Quiava Valentin Harper - opted for glammed up makeup for the event. The My Type singer showed off voluminous lashes as well as a pink, smoky shadow around her eyes to coincide with the color scheme of her outfit. A pink blush and glowy highlighter was added to better accentuate her cheekbones, while a nude-colored, glossy tint was worn on her lips and bordered by a darker lipliner. Saweetie flashed a cheerful smile as she struck a variety of poses on the pink carpet upon arriving to the Prudential Center where the festivities were held. At one point, she was seen pretending to lick the bone near the neckline of the dress. In regards to her eye-catching look for the evening, the star stated that the ensemble is 'fresh off the runway' and draws inspiration from 'caveman days' or 'fashion when it first started,' per NBC News. The beauty was seen taking to the stage and joined fellow celebrities Dometi Pongo, Nessa Diab, as well as Kevan Kenny as they hosted the pre-show event. The VMA's were held on the East Coast in Newark and the host for the ceremony was Nicki Minaj - who notably hosted last year's event as well. A number of music artists took to the stage to perform as well during the festivities, such as Anitta, Cardi B, Demi Lovato, Doja Cat, Fall Out Boy, Kelsea Ballerini, Lil Wayne, and K-pop group, Stray Kids. The night was a busy one, with special awards given to Shakira and also Sean 'Diddy' Combs during the annual ceremony. The Hips Don't Lie songstress was honored with the Michael Jackson Video Vanguard Award while Diddy was given the Global Icon Award. The VMA's also paid tribute to the 50th anniversary of hip-hop, with performances from Minaj, Lil Wayne, Grandmaster Flash and the Furious Five, and also LL Cool J. Taylor Swift led artists with the most nominations for this year's awards ceremony with a total of 11. SZA was close behind after garnering eight nominations. Saweetie has been filtering through a busy schedule and put on a glamorous display on Monday as she attended the Laquan Smith Spring/Summer 2023 runway show during New York Fashion Week - which comes to an end on September 13.
