Conor McGregor enjoys family night out at well-known Dublin pub

irishmirror 2023-09-17 17:58:38
Source page
Conor McGregor was all smiles as he enjoyed a family night out in his well-known Dublin pub The Black Forge Inn. The MMA star is a frequent visitor to his own establishment and brought his family along for the day. McGregor enjoyed steak and chips which were also accompanied by onion rings and vegetables and seemed to share a bottle of red wine with partner Dee Devlin. UFC star McGregor also shared a number of family snaps with his children as well as a photo with his friend.. Captioning a post on social media, he said: "Happy birthday baby Dove" McGregor's MMA future remains uncertain after it was announced he would fight Michael Chandler but publicly there has been no headway made on the bout happening.
